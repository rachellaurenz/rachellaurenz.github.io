Pure Vibes

This was a Generative art / Vibe Coding project where I had to include 3 three.js examples. The 3 I was assigned include: examples/webgl_shader_lava.html, examples/webgl_postprocessing_dof.html, and examples/webgl_instancing_dynamic.html

I combined the three examples by taking the shape from the lava shader and changing the shape after a specific amount of clicks. In the first area you can also see all the small shapes are in the same loop with the post processing creating multiples. The shapes are also bouncing and changing color the way they did in the instancing dynamic example.

My three parameters are:
1. Interaction: you can move around and click on the different shapes to change them
2. Sound-Reactive: after click on a shape, you will hear a sound as the shape changes
3. Time: the colors change within a specific color pallet every few seconds

Use your mouse to move around and click on the different shapes. If you scroll out far enough you will be transported to another area.
