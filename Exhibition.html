Larson Exhibition

Dr. Evan Larson came to an Indigenous club meeting at Western Michigan University and presented his work and spoke about his earlier speech "Reciprocity Through Flames". My class, IDEAS Collaboratory (ART 5000), had us show off our individual works about the subject here too. At this Exhibition, I showed off my audio "Sounds Without Chicxulub". My classmates had other diplays including vidoes, weavings, pictures, and small sculptures.
