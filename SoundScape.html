Sounds Without Chicxulub

I created this to simulate what Earth would sound like if the Chicxulub asteroid never hit Earth. I read an article that said birds probably would not have evolved to sing, which intrigued me into making this. I started with water, fire, and wind to show that those won't change. Then I added a few other sounds I recorded in the woods my dad owns.
